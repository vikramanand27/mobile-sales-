# mobile-sales-Analysis-POWERBI
# 📊 Mobile Sales Dashboard

## 📝1.project title:
An interactive **Mobile Sales Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze sales performance across different regions, brands, and time periods. The dashboard provides actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ 2.Tech Stack:
- **Microsoft Power BI** – Data visualization & dashboard creation  
- **Microsoft Excel** – Data cleaning & preprocessing  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculated measures and KPIs  

---

## 📂 3.Data Source
- Dataset collected from **online source randomly** (public datasets used for practice and learning purposes)

---

## 🚀 4.Features & Highlights

### 🔍 Business Problem
Businesses often face challenges in tracking mobile sales performance across multiple dimensions such as regions, products, and time periods. Lack of clear insights can result in inefficient strategies and missed opportunities.

---

### 🎯 5.Goal of the Dashboard
- Provide a centralized view of mobile sales data  
- Track key performance indicators (KPIs)  
- Identify top-performing products and regions  
- Enable better business decision-making  

---

### 📈 6.Walkthrough of Key Visuals
- **KPI Cards** – Total Sales, Total Quantity, Transactions, Average Sales  
- **Sales Trend (Line Chart)** – Monthly performance analysis  
- **City-wise Sales (Map/Bar Chart)** – Geographic distribution  
- **Product/Brand Analysis (Bar Chart)** – Top & bottom performers  
- **Ratings Analysis** – Customer feedback insights  
- **Category Distribution (Donut/Pie Chart)** – Sales contribution  
- **Interactive Filters/Slicers** – Dynamic user-driven insights  

---

### ❓ 7.Key Questions Answered
- What is the **total sales**?  
- What is the **total quantity sold**?  
- How many **transactions** were made?  
- What is the **average sales value**?  
- What are the **monthly sales trends**?  
- How do **customer ratings** impact sales?  
- Which **cities generate the highest sales**?  

---

### 💡 8.Business Impact & Insights
- Identified **top-performing mobile brands and cities**  
- Highlighted **monthly sales trends and seasonality**  
- Revealed **customer preferences through ratings analysis**  
- Helped detect **low-performing regions/products**  
- Enabled **data-driven strategy improvements**
